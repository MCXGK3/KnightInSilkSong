### This is a Mod for Hollow Knight Silksong

### It allows you to control the knight in the game.

### List of Known issues
- When entering a room or sequence that has a special interaction with Hornet as the Knight may cause the game to softlock
- Sometimes when going through vertical transtions as the Knight you may end up not going through the transition succesfully
- When using the Dreamgate it may cause the players camera to bug out and not be focused on the player
- When playing as the Knight if you are swimming you may not be able to jump as intended
- When swimming as the knight the player tends to sink further down instead of just floating in the water as intended
- Under certain circumstances, the Hiveblood Charm might grant an incorrect number of Hiveblood health segments. Please use with discretion.
- Under certain circumstances, The Knight's HUD might not appear. Opening and closing the Inventory should restore it.
- Under certain circumstances, Weaverlings or Grimmchild might instantly kill enemies, potentially causing softlocks. Please use them with discretion.
- Under certain circumstances, The Knight might not play the corresponding animation or Hornet's animation might appear instead. This is a normal phenomenon; please ignore it.
